Flick Support

For help with Flick, email us at useflick.app@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Common questions:

  • The extension isn't loading my listings — make sure you're logged in to Vinted and have a Vinted tab open, then click Refresh.
  • Auto-offers aren't showing likers — make sure you have a Vinted tab open and are logged in, then click refresh.
